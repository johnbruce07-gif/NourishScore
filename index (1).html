The NourishScore™

A score that rewards quality

NourishScore runs from 0 to 100 per portion. It rewards what matters — protein, fibre, whole foods — and deducts for the things that undermine your health long-term. No proprietary secrets, no opaque algorithms.

🌿
Flourish
80–100

Outstanding nutritional balance. Protein-rich, fibre-forward, low in saturates. An everyday champion.

🌱

Well-rounded with solid nutritional foundations. A reliable, satisfying everyday meal.

🌼

💫

0–19

A treat meal — savour every bite. Balance your day with lighter, nourishing choices around it.

What raises your score

+15 Protein >25g per portion
+12 Fibre >8g per portion
+8 Whole food ingredients
+6 Prominent vegetables or fruit
+5 Healthy fats (olive oil, avocado, oily fish)

What lowers your score

−1/60kcal Calories above 350 per portion
−9 Saturated fat >12g
−10 Added sugar >15g
−4 each Ultra-processed ingredients
−4 Very high sodium (>1200mg)
The Portion Framework™

The same recipe, different portions

A recipe's NourishScore measures its quality. The Portion Signal measures whether your actual serving supports your goals. Drag the portion adjuster in any recipe to see your numbers update live.

🟢
Portion-friendly

Under 450 kcal at the defined serving, with solid protein and fibre to keep you satisfied. Eat freely at this size.

🟡
Mind your portion

Calorie-dense or moderate protein. The recipe is good — consider the serving size or add a green side to the plate.

🔴
Half-and-fill

Over 650 kcal per defined serving. Try halving the portion and filling the rest of your plate with leafy greens.

The Fill Rate™ — (protein + fibre) ÷ kcal × 100 — measures satiety per calorie. Meals scoring 12+ keep you full significantly longer. All values are estimates and not a substitute for personalised dietary advice.
